Qualcomm Cloud AI 100 Playground

This platform showcases the capabilities of the Qualcomm Cloud AI 100 hardware by providing developers with essential tools and information to leverage its power. It offers demonstrations of the hardware's performance through features like chat interactions with various pre-configured AI models and text-to-image generation. Users can also monitor their model usage statistics. Furthermore, the platform provides comprehensive documentation on the Qualcomm SDK, agent, and API endpoints, enabling developers to understand and utilize the hardware for their specific use cases. Secure sign-in is facilitated via OAuth 2.0, and model credit purchases are handled through Stripe.
